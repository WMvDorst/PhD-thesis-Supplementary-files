# Supplementary Materials — PhD Thesis

**The Value of Medical Tests - To Provide the Right Care, at the Right Place, at the Right Time**

Author: Pim W.M. van Dorst
University of Groningen / University Medical Center Groningen

## About this repository

This repository contains the supplementary materials for the thesis listed above. QR codes printed in each chapter link to this repository, allowing readers to access additional documentation without expanding the printed page count of the thesis.

## Thesis structure

**Chapter 1** — General introduction

**Part 1 — Medical test innovations for NCDs: a Dutch perspective**
- **Chapter 2** — The health and economic value of Artificial Intelligence in a resource-constrained diagnostic pathway of prostate cancer in the Netherlands: a discrete event simulation model
- **Chapter 3** — Cost-effectiveness of a CYP2C19 genotype-guided strategy in acute myocardial infarction patients: results from the POPular Genetics trial
- **Chapter 4** — Prerequisites for cost-effective home blood pressure telemonitoring: early health economic analysis

**Part 2 — Medical test innovation for AMR management: an international perspective**
- **Chapter 5** — The impact of community-acquired acute respiratory tract infection: disutility estimates for seventeen European countries
- **Chapter 6** — Consultations, (antibiotic) prescriptions, and productivity losses of patients with community-acquired respiratory tract infection: country-specific findings from the PRUDENCE trial
- **Chapter 7** — Evaluations of training and education interventions for improved infectious disease management in low- and middle-income countries: a systematic literature review
- **Chapter 8** — Cost-effectiveness of test-and-treat strategies to reduce the antibiotic prescription rate for acute febrile illness in primary healthcare clinics in Africa

**Chapter 9** — General discussion
